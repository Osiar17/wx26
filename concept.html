About Worship Experience

The Map of an Experience

Why we gather—to respond to God—and the pattern every Experience follows.

Worship Experience is simply an extended praise and worship session, not a concert. It's a gathering together in God's presence to encounter Him. Everyone is invited to worship because God actively seeks those who want to worship.

Throughout the Bible, people have had encounters with God: Moses at the burning bush, Isaiah's vision of the Lord. Every true encounter was unique but they shared some patterns. Worship Experience is built around some of these patterns.

Be Engaged

James 4:8James 4:8 · ESVDraw near to God, and he will draw near to you. Cleanse your hands, you sinners, and purify your hearts, you double-minded.

Behold Him

Hebrews 11:6Hebrews 11:6 · ESVAnd without faith it is impossible to please him, for whoever would draw near to God must believe that he exists and that he rewards those who seek him.

Jeremiah 29:13Jeremiah 29:13 · ESVYou will seek me and find me, when you seek me with all your heart.

Be Transformed

1 John 3:21 John 3:2 · ESVBeloved, we are God's children now, and what we will be has not yet appeared; but we know that when he appears we shall be like him, because we shall see him as he is.

Romans 8:29Romans 8:29 · ESVFor those whom he foreknew he also predestined to be conformed to the image of his Son, in order that he might be the firstborn among many brothers.

Every encounter begins with our response. But we must deliberately turn toward God, avoiding distractions. WX exists to create space for that response, inviting every heart to turn fully toward Him.

God is not merely seen; He is revealed. We only behold Him by fixing our hearts on Him. So every song, silence, and moment is designed to hold attention on Christ and receive His revelation.

When we behold God, we see what He originally made us to be, and the Holy Spirit works within us to transform us to it. This is the goal of true worship: to leave more like Him than when we came.

Our Approach

How we serve and steward that pattern

We cannot create an encounter with God.
We only prepare the way.

The Three Convictions of Every Worship Experience

I
Every Effort Is Engagement-Driven

Every Effort Is Engagement-Driven

We cannot force a revelation of God or transform hearts, but we can create an environment where people are free to respond to Him.

Everything is therefore aimed at one goal: helping people turn toward God and remain turned toward Him. So we prioritize participation first, then pursue excellence so that neither skill nor lack of skill becomes a distraction. Everything must serve participation and true engagement.

II
Every Experience Is Theme-Driven

Every Experience Is Theme-Driven

Every Worship Experience is shaped by a single divine emphasis. The theme gives direction, shapes expectation, unifies expression, and gives the gathering one voice. Before shaping the event, it shapes us as we pray it, study it, and carry it.

From there, nothing is random. Songs, words, design, atmosphere, and communication all flow from the theme. By the time the day arrives, the experience is one unified response to what God has emphasized.

III
Everyone Present Is a Minister

Everyone Present Is a Minister

Worship Experience has no audience. Every believer is a priest before God (1 Peter 2:5, 9), and all stand as ministers unto Him. Worship is not done for the church but by the church to God.

So every decision asks: can the whole room participate? Songs are chosen for congregational voice, space is created for participation, and leaders worship first before leading. The gathering is an act of many voices and one altar—where we behold God and are transformed.

2022

2022 · The Beginning

December 18 — Worship Experience replaced Music Fest and iRequest Night.

Worship Experience was the vision of our pastor, Ps. Sammy Aduama — a lover of music who wanted an entire service dedicated to thanksgiving and worship.

It happened across both services. All we had was one all-night rehearsal — but we definitely had a desire to worship.

2023

2023 · Finding Its Identity

This time, Worship Experience came with a theme.

REGARDLESS

Habakkuk 3:17–19

To worship beyond circumstance.

This remains the only Worship Experience held mid-year. It felt like a new beginning—but something even bigger was coming.

Later that same year, Worship Experience returned.

DREAM

Psalm 126:1–3

And it was like a Dreeaaaam!

What began simply was about to expand in ways nobody expected.

Lights, LED — everything was sponsored, proudly supported by our church leadership.

We became even more intentional — because why did we sing a Chorale Medley with all its unusual parts.

A Historic First

The Dream Edition became the first Worship Experience held in a joint service. And 2023 remains the only year with two Worship Experiences — the vision was later refined into one annual gathering.
