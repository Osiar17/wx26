How to read a task

Four independent systems describe each task. They are never shown at equal weight — the status marker leads.

Status — present condition

Complete · done. Overdue · past its date, not complete. In Progress · Awaiting Approval · Blocked · At Risk · Not Started · Deferred.

Priority — importance

Critical · failure would prevent or endanger delivery. High · shapes or unlocks other work. Medium · standard preparation.

Structural role — dependency significance

Anchor · a major fixed milestone. Gate · must finish before downstream work proceeds. Standalone · does not block others.

Schedule flexibility — timing tolerance

Tight · little slack; others depend on the date. Flexible · may move within a window.
